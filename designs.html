3D Character design in Autodesk Maya

During my 3D visualisation studies in University, I undertook a project where I created 3D model replica of Avatar character Navi. This project allowed me to apply my passion for animation and develop proficiency in 3D modelling techinques.

Cultural Safe-Tree App design

As part of the Audience Perception and Experience unit, our group collaborated on a project aimed at enhancing cultural safety within our university community. My contribution involved conceptualizing an innovative app design that served dual purposes: educating users about various cultures through an interactive game while also facilitating communication through a messaging platform. The app encouraged active engagement with diverse perspectives as users navigated the campus, fostering a more inclusive and culturally aware environment.

Reverse Vending Machine

In Experience Design unit, I got dive into the design world and come up with a design that would be beneficial for the planet and it's users. My design was the new and improved Reverse Vending Machine (RVM). As a response to Perth’s ongoing container recycling challenges and our collective commitment to environmental stewardship, I conceptualized an innovative solution that merges functionality with a touch of humanity. RVM isn't just a receptacle—it's a friendly face in the recycling revolution. With its human-like features and gamified interface, interacting with the RVM becomes an engaging and rewarding experience.
