Creative designer / Web designer/ Animator / Game designer/ Artist

Hi! My name is Daisy Leming. As a creative thinker, I would like to expand my skills in a creative designer position.

I will be graduating from Edith Cowan University with a Bachelor of Design, majoring in Games and Interactive design in the end of 2024. During my coursework, I also did some web design and experience design and I am an artist by nature. My dedication to creativity and design is shown by the variety of areas that I create daily, such as traditional painting, digital art, game development and interactive animation.

In addition, I also: - Have showcased my art in Fremantle’s Old Customs House. - Showcased my art in multiple music festivals around Western Australia in 2023. - Created my portfolio website from scratch.

I know all about independent work but I enjoy working in teams too. As a designer, strategy is very important when tackling a problem. My creativity does not end there, I have also taken some units from programming side. Adobe Creative Suit apps are my go to when I need to design something. While I am currently located in Perth, I am also happy to consider relocating to Melbourne or Brisbane if I should be a successful candidate. I also don’t mind working from home as you also offer that type of work mode.
